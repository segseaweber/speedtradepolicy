Delete Account - SpeedTrade

If you wish to delete your SpeedTrade account and all associated data, please follow the steps below.

Steps to Delete Your Account

  1. Log in to your SpeedTrade app.
  2. Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account.
  3. Confirm your decision by entering your password.
  4. Submit the deletion request.

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Personal identifiers: name, email, phone number
  • Wallet addresses and transaction history
  • Payment and billing information

Data That May Be Retained

Certain data may be retained for legal, regulatory, or compliance purposes, including:

  • Financial transactions required by Nigerian law
  • Audit logs or fraud prevention records
Retained data is securely stored and not used for any other purpose.
